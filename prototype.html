How the World Changes (Easy Guide)

  • Happy squares: A colored square has 8 little spots around it. If 2 or 3 of those spots are also colored, the square stays happy (alive) next turn. If fewer than 2 friends (lonely) or more than 3 friends (crowded) are around, it disappears and we mark it with an X.
  • Baby squares: An empty spot with exactly 3 colored neighbors gets a new baby square. We show a tiny O where the baby will appear.
  • What color? Count the colors of the 3 neighbors:
    • More blues than reds ➜ baby or disappearing mark is Blue.
    • More reds than blues ➜ marker is Red.
    • Same number ➜ marker is Grey (neutral).
  • We show these X/O hints live, so you can plan your next click!
