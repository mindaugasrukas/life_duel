Life Duel Rules

Game Overview

Life Duel is a strategic two-player game based on Conway's Game of Life. Players compete by placing colored squares that evolve according to cellular automaton rules over 10 rounds.

How Turns Work

  • Blue player places 3 squares first
  • Then Red player places 3 squares
  • After both players finish, press "Evolve!"
  • Repeat for 10 rounds total

Preview Markers

  • X marks squares that will die next evolution
  • O marks empty spaces where new squares will be born
  • Use these to plan your strategy!

Evolution Rules

  • Squares with 2-3 neighbors survive
  • Squares with 0-1 or 4+ neighbors die
  • Empty spaces with exactly 3 neighbors get a new square
  • New square color depends on neighbor majority
  • Watch smooth fade animations during evolution!

Game Ending

After 10 rounds, the player with the most squares on the board wins!
