How to Play

  • Goal: Eat food to grow longer and increase your score
  • Movement: Use arrow keys or WASD to control the snake
  • Food: Red squares are regular food (+10 points)
  • Special Food: Orange squares give bonus points (+25 points)
  • Avoid: Don't hit walls or your own tail
  • Growth: Snake grows longer each time you eat food
Strategy Tips:
  • Plan your path - avoid boxing yourself in corners
  • Use the edges carefully - they can trap you quickly
  • Stay calm as you get longer - more planning needed
  • Special food appears randomly - worth the risk!
  • Higher difficulty = more points per food

Scoring System

  • Regular Food: 10 × difficulty multiplier
  • Special Food: 25 × difficulty multiplier
  • Difficulty Multipliers:
    • Easy: 1×
    • Medium: 1.5×
    • Hard: 2×
    • Insane: 3×
